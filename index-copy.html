Report

21st to 23rd September 2023 at Chennai Trade Center, Chennai

IRMRA 24th RUBBER CONFERENCE & EXPO

Indian Rubber Manufactures Research Association (IRMRA), an autonomous body under the aegis of Department for Promotion of Industry & Internal Trade (DPIIT), celebrated it's 65 Foundation year during 21 to 23 September 2023 at Chennai Trade Center, Chennai. On this eve, IRMRA organised 24 edition of its Rubber Conference & Exhibition.
